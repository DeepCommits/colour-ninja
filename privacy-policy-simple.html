Colour Ninja - Privacy Policy

TL;DR: We collect minimal analytics data to improve the extension. All your color data stays on your device. We don't share personal information.

What We Collect

  • Analytics (Anonymous): Feature usage, performance metrics, user preferences
  • Local Data: Your color palettes, settings (stored on your device only)

How We Use It

  • Improve extension performance and user experience
  • Fix bugs and add new features
  • Provide better tool recommendations

Data Storage

  • Color Data: Stored locally on your device only
  • Analytics: Sent to Google Analytics (anonymous, aggregated)
  • No Personal Data: We don't collect names, emails, or personal information

Your Rights

  • Clear all data anytime through Chrome settings
  • Disable analytics by blocking extension network access
  • All color data remains under your control
